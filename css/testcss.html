The End Of The F***ing World

The End of the F***ing World is a British dark comedy-drama television programme, based on the graphic novel The End of the Fucking World by Charles S. Forsman. The first episode of the eight-part programme premiered on Channel 4 in the United Kingdom on 24 October 2017, after which the entire programme was released on All 4. The End of the F***ing World was exclusively released by Netflix internationally on 5 January 2018. The programme jointly follows James (Alex Lawther), a 17-year-old who believes himself to be a psychopath, and Alyssa (Jessica Barden), a rebellious classmate who sees in James a chance to escape from her tumultuous home life. Since its premiere, the programme has received critical acclaim, with the writing, Lawther and Barden's performances and the programme's execution and subject matter garnering praise.
